This e-learning module serves to inform city leadership about the significance of using competition based approach and motivating ULB’s revenue officials (including tax collectors), with an aim to ultimately achieve improved own revenues at the ULB and usher in data-driven performance management for revenue officials. At the end of this module, the user would have understood how to improve property tax system at the ULB, and implement a competition for its tax/revenue officials. The module also includes case studies of other Indian cities where such competition-based initiatives have resulted in increased collection of taxes & other own revenues, including from digital modes of payment.
