How it works

  • Urban Local Bodies to Upload Data

    All ULBs are required to submit their key financial data and upload their financial documents on the www.cityfinance.in platform.

  • Independent PMU Team Verifies Data

    Independent Project Management Unit team reviews, verifies and approves data.

  • Computation of Scores

    Computation of scores and ranks based on assessment indicators and scoring methodology.

  • Final Ranking Published

    Final results (scores and ranks) will be published on www.cityfinance.in results dashboard, powered by advanced data analytics and visualization tools.

About city finance.in
The City Finance platform serves as a national framework of standardized, timely and credible financial information on India’s cities (urban local bodies).
The platform hosts more than 8,400 audited annual accounts of nearly 3,000 municipalities (ULBs) in a standardized and comparable format.
