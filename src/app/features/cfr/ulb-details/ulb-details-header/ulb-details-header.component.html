About City Finance Rankings

The CityFinance Rankings, an initiative of the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs in collaboration with Janaagraha, evaluates the financial health of India's cities (Urban Local Bodies ULBs) based on their financial performance across various parameters. By promoting fiscal accountability and transparency among ULBs, the rankings ultimately aim to improve the quality of life for residents across India's urban landscape.
